Adrift

By Tanya Guerrero

Book Summary

Adrift is about two cousins, Coral and Isa, who are super close. They live on Pebble Island, but then Coral’s family goes on a sailing trip in Indonesia. Everything goes wrong when an earthquake hits and a giant tidal wave sinks their boat. Coral ends up stuck on a random island with her dog, Bambi. She’s really sad about her parents, but she figures out how to survive. Back home, Isa’s sure Coral’s still alive and keeps hoping. The story switches between them, and it’s all about staying strong and family.

Main Characters

Coral Bituin-Rousseau: She’s 13 and pretty tough. After the boat sinks, she’s stranded with Bambi on this empty island. She learns to fish and build stuff, even though she’s super upset about losing her parents.

Isa Bituin-Rousseau: Isa’s 12 and Coral’s cousin. She stays on Pebble Island and won’t give up on Coral. She writes letters and tries to get people to keep looking for her.

Bambi: He’s Coral’s dog and survives the wreck with her. He helps her find food and keeps her company while they’re stuck.

The Story

Coral and Isa are best buds on Pebble Island until Coral’s family sails off. Then an earthquake makes a huge tidal wave that wrecks their boat. Coral and Bambi get washed up on an island, and she has to learn how to survive while dealing with her family being gone. Meanwhile, Isa’s back home writing letters and telling everyone Coral’s still out there. The book goes back and forth between them, and it ends with a big reunion that shows how much they care about each other.

My Thoughts

I think Adrift is really good! Coral’s survival stuff is exciting, and Isa’s hope makes it sweet. Bambi’s awesome too—I kinda wanted more of him in the story. I’d give it a 9.9 out of 10 because it’s fun to read and makes you feel stuff. You should totally check it out if you like books about tough times and family.
